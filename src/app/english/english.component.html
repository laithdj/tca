English Programmes

Learning English in New Zealand is easy with tailored programmes:
  • English courses in New Zealand are flexible and you can start at any time to suit your needs.
  • English courses are designed to help you meet international English standards.
  • Learning English in New Zealand will prepare you for future study and life in NZ and around the world.
List of popular English Courses to study in New Zealand:

General English

If you want to improve your English to help you communicate, work or travel, this course is for you. It covers reading, writing, listening and speaking at all levels, from beginner to advanced.

IELTS and TOEFL courses

If you need to use English as the main language of communication for work or study, these courses will help you get there. They will assess your proficiency and get you ready for the IELTS or TOEFL exams.

Cambridge Exam preparation

This course develops your language and gets you ready for the Cambridge Exams, which are accepted by universities, employers and governments around the world.

Academic English

If English is not your first language and you want to pathway to further study in New Zealand, an Academic English or English for Academic Purposes (EAP) course is ideal. It develops your English and other important skills such as writing essays and reports, listening, discussing and reading effectively.

English + Work

Combine studying English with real work experience including Demi au Pair and Internships.
