The University of Waikato focuses on the quality of our teaching and the learner experience; with smaller class sizes, students receive greater flexibility and support from their lecturers.
The university fosters a collaborative environment through workshops and tutorials as part of the learning process, and the engagement of students on campus in vibrant learning environments is a key aspect of their learning experience.
Their flexible degree structures mean students can study a broad range of subjects to shape a qualification that matches their strengths and career interests.
The university makes sure all their students are industry-ready through Work Integrated Learning modules. This hands-on experience with potential future employers ensures students stand out to future employers.
